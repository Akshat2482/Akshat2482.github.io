Surface AR

Palm Grab Cube

Start AR, scan for a real surface, then tap the screen or press Place Cube. The cube idles with a float-spin animation until you grab it with your palm and non-index fingertips.

Checking WebXR support.
Grab gesture: move your palm near the cube, then curl middle, ring, or pinky fingertips toward your palm center. Native WebXR hand joints are used when the browser/device exposes them.
